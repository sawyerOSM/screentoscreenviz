WMTO Screen to Screen Network


The purpose of this data visualization is to convey team architecture, budget data, FTE data, team functions, and subteam communication within the Warner Media Technology & Operations Department. Below are some details about the visualization:


  • The teams are arranged from the production stage (left) to the consumer platform stage (right).
  • The size of each node is determined by the FTE counts.
  • The brightness of each node is determined by its non-labor budget spend. The deeper the color conveys a higher budget.
  • When hovering over a team node, edges will appear to indicate which teams work with together. Hovering over subteam nodes will connect specific subteams that work together.
  • You can scroll to zoom and drag to move around the visualization. Click on any node for more details
